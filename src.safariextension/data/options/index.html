Settings

YouTube HD always tries to set the video quality to the highest available one. This might not be your preference if video quality is already acceptable.

YouTube HD always selects the highest available quality if your preferred one is not available.
Misc

In case you open many YouTube instances simultaneously, this extension prevents YouTube from auto-buffering and hence reduces your bandwidth usage, and helps the current video be more responsive.

YouTube HD opens the welcome page on each upgrade to notify users about their recent update. If this is not your preferred behavior you can disable it.
